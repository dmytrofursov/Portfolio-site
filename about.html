ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Dmytro, and I'm currently studying Industrial Manufacturing Technologies at Politechincal University of Turin. My path into robotics began in High School when two unknown to me professors called me to try to participate in Worldskills Nationals in "Robot System Integration", sponsored by FANUC. There my passion started. I managed to win the nationals in 2022 and 2023 by gaining the pass to participate in European and World level competitions. During the preparation I had the oportunity to do many weeks of trainings in FANUC and even to participate in an international training in FANUC France.

Currently, I'm aiming to expand my automation knowledge in multidivisional sense. From PLC and HMI programming to Addittive Manufacturing and Materials.
